# E-Commerce Sales, Profit & Customer Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## Short Description
This Power BI project analyzes **sales performance, profit drivers, and customer behavior** for an e-commerce business.  
For example, the dashboard helps answer questions such as:
- Which products and countries contribute the most profit?
- How volatile are daily sales?
- Who are the top customers by revenue and order value?
- How do new vs returning customers behave?

---

## Tech Stack / Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop  
- Power Query (data cleaning & transformation)  
- DAX (KPIs, profit margin, averages, aggregations)  
- Data Modeling  
- Interactive slicers and filters  

---

## Data Source
- Dataset sourced from **Kaggle**
- Public e-commerce dataset containing orders, customers, products, sales, profit, ratings, and order status information

---

## Features & Highlights

### Business Problem
An e-commerce business needs a unified view to:
- Track sales, profit, and margins
- Identify key revenue and profit drivers
- Understand customer contribution and retention
- Monitor order fulfillment and daily sales volatility  

Without this visibility, decision-making around pricing, inventory, and customer strategy becomes inefficient.

---

### Goal of the Dashboard
- Provide a high-level sales and profit overview
- Identify top-performing products, customers, and countries
- Analyze order fulfillment status and sales trends
- Understand customer behavior, retention, and feedback  

---

### Key Visuals Walkthrough
- **KPI Cards:** Total Sales Amount, Total Stock Quantity, Average Unit Price, Total Profit, Profit Margin %, Order Count  
- **Donut Chart:** Stock quantity distribution by product category  
- **Bar Charts:**  
  - Profit contribution by country  
  - Profit by product  
  - Top customers by sales and average order value  
- **Line Chart:** Daily sales trend highlighting volatility  
- **Order Status Panel:** Delivered, Shipped, Pending, Cancelled orders  
- **Customer Analytics:**  
  - New vs Returning customers  
  - Customer purchase frequency  
  - Customer ratings and review text analysis  
- **Geographic Analysis:** Customer distribution by country  

---

### Business Impact & Insights
- A small set of products and customers contribute a large share of total profit  
- Certain countries consistently outperform others in profit contribution  
- Daily sales show noticeable volatility, indicating demand fluctuations  
- Returning customers form a significant portion of revenue, highlighting retention value  
- Higher-rated products align with stronger sales and repeat purchases  

These insights help guide **pricing strategy, inventory planning, customer targeting, and operational decisions**.
